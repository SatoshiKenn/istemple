Reception Information

We know your reception is a really special and important moment. That's why we offer you all of these things to make that moment memorable!

Wedding reception

We offer a place to do your reception and decorate the place with a great variety of colors, when you are planning with us we can arrange all the decoration.

Food options

Cakes

We offer different sizes for the cake, small, medium and large. You can choose the flavor you want and if you have an idea of how you want your cake you can tell us!

Cocktail

Cocktail for sure it will always be a good option as main dish, in the planning you can tell us how many people will be with you in the reception and with that information we can give you values of the different cocktails we have to offer.

Buffet

Buffet as the main dish is a great option if you want a little more of food for the guests, the buffet meal consist of several dishes from which guests serve themselves.
